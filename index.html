ABOUT
-----
Greetings! I'm Scott, a dedicated software engineer with a specialized focus on backend development. With over eight years of experience in the dynamic field of technology, I excel at crafting robust solutions to intricate problems. My professional journey has led me through diverse projects, including websites, servers, backend data pipelines, and embedded systems. From database architecture, highly concurrent servers, to optimizing performance, I find joy in creating seamless and efficient software experiences.

Beyond the coding realm, I'm an avid runner who embraces the physical and mental challenges that come with each stride. Running serves as my personal reset button, allowing me to recharge and enhance my creativity.

When I'm not immersed in lines of code, you'll find me immersed in the pages of a good book. Whether fiction, non-fiction, or tech-related literature, I'm a firm believer in the power of continuous learning. Gaming is another passion of mine, providing a platform for both relaxation and collaboration. Exploring virtual worlds and solving intricate puzzles are sources of inspiration and enjoyment. Photography is my chosen medium for visual storytelling. From capturing breathtaking landscapes to the essence of bustling city life, I enjoy sharing my unique perspective through the lens.

Join me on this multifaceted journey where the precision of code meets the rhythm of running, the depth of literature, the thrill of gaming, and the artistry of photography. Let's explore the fascinating intersection of technology and life together!
